Frosting Frosting is a sweet, spreadable topping used to decorate and fill desserts. It typically combines butter and sugar, whipped together until a light and fluffy texture is achieved. This sweet spread adds a finishing touch to cakes, cookies and pastries and can be flavored and customized to your taste. Frosting ingredients vary by recipe and the type of frosting you are creating. However, frosting typically consists of sweetener, liquids like milk or egg whites and fats like butter, coconut oil, shortening or cream cheese. There are several different types of frosting you can create and experiment with to find the right match for your favorite dessert recipe.
